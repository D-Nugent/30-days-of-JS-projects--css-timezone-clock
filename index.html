Timezone Clock

Local Time:

Hi there, welcome to my regional timezone converter. To get started simply choose a region from the dropdown and click 'Add Clock'.

Rather than calculating the difference in hours between timezones I opted instead to convert the time via localString options; this ensures that the clocks account for daylight savings.
